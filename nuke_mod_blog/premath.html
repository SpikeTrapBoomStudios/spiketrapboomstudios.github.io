II: Sin and Cos Basics

Sin and Cos are two trigonometric functions, that when you are dealing with circles, it is the best way to do stuff. Its not too complex, just think of a circle with a radius of 1, aka a unit circle. Now take any angle (in radians) that starts pointing at (1,0) and then goes counter clockwise, and Sin will return the Y-Value while Cos will return the X-Value.
